Tools

Tools for thinking, building, and creating across disciplines.

FlowTools.directory — A meta-list of tools for thought, knowledge management, and creative workflows. Maintained collaboratively. META-LIST
Dynamicland — Bret Victor’s communal computing lab. Computation leaves screens and enters physical, shared space. COMPUTING
IdeaFlow — Knowledge graph notebook that augments intelligence as you think. Notes that become thinking partners. KNOWLEDGE
Are.na — Visual bookmarking and research tool for connecting ideas. The thinking person’s Pinterest. RESEARCH
Readings

Essays, books, and deep dives that shaped how we think about building across boundaries.

IdeaFlow Background — Jacob Cole’s curated reading list on knowledge tools, augmenting intelligence, and the history of tools for thought. GUIDE
Inventing on Principle — Bret Victor’s foundational talk. Creators need an immediate connection to what they create. TALK
Age of Entanglement — Neri Oxman’s Krebs Cycle of Creativity — mapping the four domains (Science, Engineering, Design, Art) and the flows between them. ESSAY
Transformative Tools for Thought — Andy Matuschak & Michael Nielsen on what it would take to build media as transformative as the Hindu-Arabic numeral system. ESSAY
Why Books Don’t Work — Andy Matuschak on why transmissionist media fails and what might replace it. ESSAY
Home-Cooked Software — Maggie Appleton on the coming golden age of personal software by non-professional engineers. ESSAY
Pace Layering — Stewart Brand on how complex systems learn. Fashion moves fast, nature moves slow. One of the most cited frameworks in systems thinking. ESSAY
Childhoods of Exceptional People — Henrik Karlsson on the environments that produced original thinkers. Tutored 1-on-1, surrounded by talented adults, enormous unstructured time. ESSAY
Adjacent communities

Other spaces where cross-disciplinary builders gather.

The SF Commons — Patricia Mou’s “fourth place” in Hayes Valley, SF. A space for meaning-making, embodied pluralism, and non-dogmatic ritual. SPACE
Long Now Foundation — Fostering long-term thinking. The 10,000-year clock, the Rosetta Project, and seminars about long-term responsibility. FOUNDATION
Interdependence — A literary-hacker community reimagining digital futures through collective authorship. Co-signed by Daanish Shabbir and others. COLLECTIVE
